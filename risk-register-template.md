## Field Guidance

- Asset / Process: system, workflow, data type, or business process affected.
- Threat: what could go wrong.
- Vulnerability: weakness or gap that makes the threat more likely.
- Likelihood: expected probability based on exposure and controls.
- Impact: business, operational, compliance, or security effect.
- Recommendation: specific next action that can be assigned and verified.
