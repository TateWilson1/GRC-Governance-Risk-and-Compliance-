# Control Mapping Notes

## CIS Controls

- Account Management: stale account review, local admin review, and owner documentation.
- Access Control Management: least privilege, privileged role review, and approval evidence.
- Audit Log Management: evidence of review, retention, and escalation paths.

## NIST CSF

- Identify: asset, user, and process ownership.
- Protect: access control, MFA, policy, and hardening actions.
- Detect: logging, monitoring, and review signals.
- Respond: documented owners, communication paths, and remediation tracking.

## Compliance Lens

- HIPAA: access to protected health information and evidence of safeguards.
- FERPA: access to student records and education data handling.
- PCI DSS: cardholder-data access, segmentation, logging, and control evidence.
