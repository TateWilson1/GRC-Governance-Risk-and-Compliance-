# Sample Executive Summary

The simulated organization showed elevated risk from inconsistent access review, weak documentation, and limited evidence of control ownership. The highest-priority concern was identity and data access because stale accounts, unclear ownership, or excessive permissions can increase the likelihood of unauthorized access and slow incident response.

Recommended near-term actions:

- Run an access review for privileged and sensitive-data users.
- Remove stale accounts and document approved exceptions.
- Assign owners for recurring access review and evidence collection.
- Track remediation in a risk register until closure.
