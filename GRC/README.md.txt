Title: GRC Portfolio Project: Applied Risk Assessments 
Author: Tate Wilson

Project Overview This repository contains three independent mock risk assessments demonstrating proficiency in different compliance frameworks (FERPA, HIPAA, PCI-DSS). Each assessment simulates a specific Level-2 GRC Analyst engagement, focusing on unique environments, threat landscapes, and regulatory requirements.

Competencies Demonstrated

Framework Application: Translating legal/standard requirements (FERPA, HIPAA, PCI) into technical controls.

Risk Analysis: Utilizing qualitative risk matrices to prioritize threats based on Likelihood and Impact.

Business Communication: Writing Executive Summaries and Business Impact Narratives suitable for stakeholders.

Scenarios

Academic Environment: Securing Residence Hall networks under FERPA.

Healthcare Environment: Protecting small clinic PHI under HIPAA Security Rule.

E-Commerce Environment: Managing outsourced payment risks under PCI-DSS (SAQ-A).
